About Us

Introduction

Jaxion originally started in February of 2018 as the Krampus Community, Discore set to help those who have mental health issues build confidence and social skills through gaming. In March, Jaxion stepped down and turned the community over to Haeria.

Haeria set out to create a safe and loving environment for the community.
